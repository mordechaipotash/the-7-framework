## Moving GUT from EMPTY toward STEADY

*For: depleted, can't start, crashed, no energy*

### Sleep Hygiene

**Moves**: GUT from empty toward steady
**Type**: lifestyle
**Evidence**: research
**Source**: Walker, "Why We Sleep"; numerous sleep-depression studies

**What it is**:
Consistent sleep/wake times, dark room, no screens before bed, cool temperature.

**How it works**:
Sleep is when energy systems restore. Chronic deficit keeps GUT depleted regardless of other interventions.

**Notes**:
Foundational. Fix this before expecting other interventions to work. Often overlooked.
