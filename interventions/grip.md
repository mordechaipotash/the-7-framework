## Moving GRIP from TIGHT toward BALANCED

*For: rumination, obsessive thoughts, can't let go, worry loops*

### Cognitive Defusion (ACT)

**Moves**: GRIP from tight toward balanced
**Type**: cognitive
**Evidence**: research
**Source**: Hayes et al., Acceptance and Commitment Therapy literature

**What it is**:
Rather than fighting thoughts, observe them as thoughts. "I notice I'm having the thought that..." Label rather than engage.

**How it works**:
Loosens identification with thoughts. Creates space between you and the content. Reduces grip by reducing struggle.

**Notes**:
Different from thought stopping (which often tightens GRIP). Works better for sticky thoughts than for true compulsions.
