## Moving REACH from MERGED toward CONNECTED

*For: enmeshed, can't exist without others, no boundaries*

### Boundary Setting Practice

**Moves**: REACH from merged toward connected
**Type**: behavioral/cognitive
**Evidence**: clinical
**Source**: DBT, various relationship therapy modalities

**What it is**:
Practice saying no to small things. Notice the discomfort. Tolerate it without rescuing.

**How it works**:
Builds evidence that relationship survives limits. Differentiates self from other.

**Notes**:
Start small. Large boundary attempts often fail and reinforce merging patterns.
