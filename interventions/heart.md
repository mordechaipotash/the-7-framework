## Moving HEART from NUMB toward ALIVE

### Behavioral Activation

**Moves**: HEART from numb toward alive
**Type**: behavioral
**Evidence**: research
**Source**: Martell et al., "Behavioral Activation for Depression"

**What it is**:
Schedule and complete activities regardless of motivation. Start with previously enjoyable activities, even if they don't feel appealing now.

**How it works**:
Breaks the withdrawal-numbness cycle. Action precedes motivation, not the other way around.

**Notes**:
Most effective when done consistently despite lack of initial feeling. Feeling often follows weeks later.
