# The 7: Body Map Visual Representation

## Core Concept

The 7 characteristics are mapped to where people actually FEEL them in their body. This isn't arbitrary - it connects the abstract framework to lived, felt experience.

---

## Body Location Map

| Char | Body Location | Why There |
|------|---------------|-----------|
| HEAD | Head, behind the eyes | Where attention "points from" |
| GUARD | Chest, shoulders, throat | Where anxiety grips - heart races, breath catches, shoulders rise |
| HEART | Heart center | Where aliveness/deadness is felt - "heavy heart" vs "light" |
| GUT | Core, belly, solar plexus | Where energy reserves live - "gutted" when depleted |
| GRIP | Hands, jaw, shoulders | Where we literally hold/clench |
| GROUND | Feet, base of spine | Where groundedness is felt - rooted vs floating |
| REACH | Arms | Reaching out vs pulling in |

## Why Body Mapping Works

1. **It's YOUR body** - not an abstract chart
2. **You already feel these things** - just naming what's there
3. **It connects thinking to feeling** - bridges the gap
4. **It's memorable** - you carry your body everywhere
5. **It invites checking in** - "what's happening in my chest right now?"
6. **It's universal** - everyone has a body, regardless of language/culture

---

## Check-In Practice

Quick daily/weekly body scan:

```
Close your eyes. Breathe.

HEAD     - Where's my attention right now?
GUARD    - Any tension in my chest, shoulders, throat?
HEART    - Does my heart feel open or closed? Alive or flat?
GUT      - How's my energy tank?
GRIP     - Am I clenching anywhere? Jaw? Hands? Holding tight to thoughts?
GROUND   - Do my feet feel connected to the floor? Am I HERE?
REACH    - Am I reaching out or pulling in today?
```

Seven questions. 30 seconds. Body already has the answers.
