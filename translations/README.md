3. `/README.md` — Overview

### Cultural adaptation notes

The 7 uses body metaphors that may need adaptation:

- **HEAD**: "Where's your head at?" common in English
- **GUARD**: Military/protection metaphor
- **HEART**: "Heavy heart" vs "light heart" may vary
- **GUT**: "Gut feeling" may not translate directly
- **GRIP**: "Let it go" / "holding on" metaphors
- **GROUND**: "Grounded" / "ungrounded" may need explanation
- **REACH**: "Reaching out" as connection metaphor

Feel free to adapt metaphors to local idiom while preserving meaning.
